Overview

Established in 1994, Yashwantrao Mohite Institute of Management, Karad is one of the constituent units of Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed to be University, Pune. The institute is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Govt. of Maharashtra.

The Institute has a high reputation in the academic sphere, and, since its establishment, it has continued to contribute to producing the best management professionals for the industry.

Due to its reputation, the institute attracts students from all over the country. It operates two world-class departments – Management, and Computer Applications. The students learn from a team of committed and competent faculty and non-teaching staff.

Yashwantrao Mohite Institute of Management is located in Karad, which is a picturesque and quiet locality that provides an appropriate ambience for academic pursuits. The institute is located in a spacious complex having a built-up area of 4059.5 sq.m. A well-equipped library with books and national, international journals, built over an area of more than 1000 sq. ft., is accessible to all of the institute’s students and staff.
